Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 14, 2026

GroshiYe (ГрошіЄ) is a personal expense tracking app built for Ukraine. Our principle is simple: your financial data belongs to you. We do not sell data, show ads, or track you across the internet.

1. Data we collect

  • Account: email and name (with Apple or Google sign-in — only what you allow to share). Passwords are stored as hashes only.
  • monobank transactions: only if you connect your bank yourself by confirming access in the monobank app (the bank's official partner API, read-only access to statements and account names). We never see your banking credentials and cannot move money.
  • Receipt data: when you scan a fiscal receipt QR code, we fetch the receipt contents (store, items, amounts) from the open registry of the State Tax Service of Ukraine.
  • Your settings: categories, budgets, notes — everything you create in the app.

2. How we use data

  • To show you your own spending, reports and budgets. That's it.
  • Item names from receipts (without amounts, your name, or any personal data) are processed by an AI service for automatic categorization.
  • Push notifications (reminders, budget alerts) are sent only if you enable them.

3. Where data is stored

On Supabase servers in the eu-central-1 (Frankfurt, EU) data center. Connections are TLS-encrypted; data is encrypted at rest. Bank access keys never leave the server.

4. Who we share data with

We do not sell personal data and do not share it with ad networks. Service providers we rely on:

  • Supabase — database and authentication (EU);
  • Apple — push notifications and subscription processing;
  • RevenueCat — subscription status (no payment data — Apple handles payments);
  • monobank — bank statements, with your explicit consent;
  • State Tax Service of Ukraine — receipt lookup by QR code;
  • Anthropic — categorization of item names (item names only, no personal data).

5. Retention and deletion

Data is kept while your account exists. You can delete everything in the app: Profile → Delete account — your account, transactions, receipts and settings are erased immediately and permanently. Bank access can be revoked separately in the monobank app.

6. Your rights

You may access, correct, or delete your data, and withdraw consent at any time — in the app or by emailing yeghroshi@gmail.com.

7. Age

Bank connection requires age 16+ (a monobank API requirement).

8. Changes

If this policy changes, we will update the date above and notify about material changes in the app.
